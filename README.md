# Partenaire

## Introduction
Partenaire is an application designed to help users find and connect with business partners across various industries. It simplifies the process of networking and collaboration by providing a user-friendly platform to meet potential partners, share resources, and develop projects together.

## Features
- **User Profiles:** Create detailed profiles to showcase your skills, experiences, and business goals.
- **Matching Algorithm:** Utilizes a sophisticated algorithm to match you with potential partners who share similar interests and objectives.
- **Messaging System:** Communicate directly with potential partners within the app.
- **Project Collaboration:** Start projects and invite collaborators, manage tasks, and share files securely.
